Surigao City and Marinduque

Surigao City

Surigao City, designated as the City of Surigao,
holds the status of a first-class component city and serves as the capital of Surigao del Norte province in the Philippines.
As per the 2020 census, its population is recorded at 171,107 individuals

Marinduque

Marinduque, officially recognized as the Province of Marinduque,
stands as an island province situated in the Philippines within the Southwestern Tagalog Region or Mimaropa, formerly known as Region IV-B.
The municipality of Boac serves as its capital. Positioned between Tayabas Bay to the north and the Sibuyan Sea to the south, Marinduque boasts a distinctive geographical location.
